AI Privacy Rights

Privacy Rights in the Age of AI

In the AI era, protecting privacy is more important—and challenging—than ever. AI relies on vast amounts of personal data, like location, browsing habits, and even biometric details. While this enables amazing innovations, it also raises concerns about surveillance, data misuse, and bias.

Teenagers, children, and vulnerable groups are particularly at risk, as AI systems can exploit or unfairly target them. Laws like the GDPR aim to protect individuals, but as AI evolves, governments and companies must ensure these technologies respect privacy while remaining ethical and fair.

Maintaining privacy in the digital age is key to building trust and security in a world shaped by AI.
