Real-World Applications of AI

Healthcare

AI technologies are being used to improve patient outcomes through predictive analytics, personalized medicine, and robotic surgeries.

Finance

In finance, AI algorithms analyze market trends and assist in fraud detection, risk management, and automated trading.

Transportation

AI is revolutionizing transportation with self-driving cars, traffic management systems, and predictive maintenance for vehicles.

Retail

Retailers use AI for inventory management, personalized shopping experiences, and customer service chatbots.
